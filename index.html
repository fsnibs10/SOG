Schizosaccharomyces orthogroup web resource
A Schizosaccharomyces orthogroup (SOG) is a set of Schizosaccharomyces genes sharing a common ancestry.
A total of 5400 SOGs have been defined based on the protein-coding genes in the genomes of the reference strains of S. pombe, S. pombe CN lineage, S. cryophilus, S. octosporus, S. lindneri, S. osmophilus, S. osmophilus CA lineage, S. japonicus, and S. versatilis.
Click one of the buttons below to access a searchable SOG list, which provides entry points for sequence alignment, phylogenetic tree, or synteny plot for each SOG.
